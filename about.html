About

Trip planning should feel like the trip has already started.

Tripva turns a short travel idea into a complete day-by-day plan: route, hotels, food, tickets, budget, transport, photo spots, and reminders. It is built for travellers who do not want another blank document, another spreadsheet, or twenty open tabs.

The product is operated by Futurizta Tech Sdn Bhd and is designed as a practical travel dashboard first: generate a plan, edit it, save it, share it, and use it while moving.
