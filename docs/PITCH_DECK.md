# TripHQ Pitch Deck (Draft)
_Last updated: 2026-03-25_

## Slide 1 — Cover
- TripHQ
- "Your trip, headquarters."
- AI trip operating system for modern travelers

## Slide 2 — Problem
- Trip planning is fragmented across search, maps, hotels, trains, tickets, and notes.
- Average traveler context-switches across 10+ tabs and apps.
- Existing tools either plan only, or track only; they do not operate the trip end-to-end.

## Slide 3 — Solution
- TripHQ generates a complete itinerary from a short prompt.
- Users get one mobile-first dashboard during travel: Live, Trip, Days, Tickets, Book Now, Budget.
- Built-in booking actions reduce planning friction and create immediate monetization.

## Slide 4 — Product
- Input: destination, dates, style, budget, group size.
- Output: full day-by-day itinerary with map, stays, transport, food, activities, reservations.
- Shareable trip URL + PWA behavior for in-trip usage.

## Slide 5 — Market
- Global travel market: ~$1.7T (industry-wide travel spend benchmark).
- Initial wedge: English-speaking millennial leisure travelers (25-40).
- Expansion: group planning, creators, travel agencies, white-label B2B.

## Slide 6 — Business Model
- Affiliate-first revenue:
  - Hotels (Booking affiliate links)
  - Activities (GetYourGuide partner links)
  - Trains and transport links
- Freemium SaaS upsell:
  - Pro: unlimited generation + collaboration + exports
  - Business: team seats + white-label + API

## Slide 7 — Traction (Current)
- Working production web app and backend on Vercel.
- End-to-end generation flow validated (including streaming plan build).
- Booking links generated with affiliate tracking parameters in live payloads.
- Initial launch assets and GTM plan completed.

## Slide 8 — Why Now
- Consumers now trust AI for high-intent planning workflows.
- Travel booking behavior has moved mobile-first and intent-rich.
- Affiliate infrastructure makes early monetization possible before full subscription scale.

## Slide 9 — Competitive Positioning
- Not a chatbot-only planner.
- Not a static itinerary doc.
- TripHQ is an operating layer: plan + execute + book + track in one workflow.
