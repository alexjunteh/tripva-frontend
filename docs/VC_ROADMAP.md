## Warm Intro Strategy
_Cold emails have ~2% response rate. Warm intros have ~40%. Priority: find a path to warm._

### How to get warm intros
1. **LinkedIn**: Find someone who works at the target fund → connect with a genuine message → ask if they'd be open to seeing a deck
2. **YC alumni network**: If you get YC interview, you get access to alumni Slack → ask for intros
3. **Portfolio founders**: Find founders at companies the VC has backed → ask for an intro
4. **AngelList / Twitter**: Many seed VCs are very active on Twitter/X. Build a public build-in-public thread about Roam → they find you
5. **ProductHunt**: A strong ProductHunt launch gets VCs into your inbox. Time it right (Tuesday 12:01am PT)

### Cold Email Template (under 100 words)
```
Subject: Roam — AI trip OS, $3.20 affiliate rev/trip, live in 10 days

Hi [Name],

I built Roam in 10 days while on a trip in Italy — because trip planning takes 16 hours and nobody has fixed it.

Roam generates a complete, bookable trip dashboard in 60 seconds, with affiliate booking links (Booking.com, GYG, Trainline) wired in from day 1.

We're raising $500k seed. Live app: [URL]. Happy to send the deck.

Alex
```
