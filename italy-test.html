💡 Tips
✈️Before You Fly
▾
›Passport valid 6+ months beyond return date
›Check visa requirements for your destination
›Download Google Maps offline before departure
›Notify your bank of travel dates
›Travel insurance — get it, always
›Photo your passport, email it to yourself
💰Money & Cards
▾
›Always carry local cash — cards fail at small spots
›Wise or Revolut: best exchange rates, no fees
›Airport ATMs = worst rates. Use city bank ATMs.
›Load your destination currency before you go
📱Tech & Connectivity
▾
›Pocket WiFi or local SIM — decide before landing
›Power bank 10,000mAh minimum for full days
›Download Grab, Uber or local taxi app in advance
›Screenshot everything important — offline backup
