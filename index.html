Travel Essentials 💡
✈️Before You Fly
•Passport: valid 6+ months beyond return date
•Check visa requirements for your destination
•Download Google Maps offline for your destinations
•Notify your bank of travel dates to avoid card blocks
•Travel insurance: get it, use it
•Photo your passport + email to yourself
💰Money & Budget
•Carry some local cash — cards fail at small restaurants
•Use a no-FX-fee card (Wise/Revolut) for best rates
•ATMs at the airport usually have worst rates — use city ATMs
•Keep USD/EUR as backup emergency cash
📱Tech & Connectivity
•Pocket WiFi or local SIM — decide before landing
•Power bank: minimum 10,000mAh for full day trips
•Universal travel adapter if visiting multiple regions
•Download Uber/Grab/local taxi app before arriving
🎒Packing Smart
•Roll clothes, don't fold — saves 30% space
•One carry-on only for trips under 10 days if possible
•Packing cubes are genuinely life-changing
•Slip-on shoes for countries where shoe removal is common
🚨Emergency
•Save local emergency number (112 in EU, 110/119 in Japan)
•Know your country's embassy address at the destination
•Keep RM500+ emergency cash separate from main wallet
•Share itinerary with someone back home
