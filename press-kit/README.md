# Tripva Press Kit

## One-liner

Tripva is an AI trip planner that turns a destination idea into a complete itinerary dashboard in 30 seconds.

## Short Description

Tripva helps travellers generate structured day-by-day itineraries with hotels, activities, transport, food, tickets, budget notes, maps, booking links, and live travel alerts.

## Boilerplate

Tripva is an AI travel planning product operated by Futurizta Tech Sdn Bhd. It is built for travellers who want a usable plan, not a long text answer: the generated itinerary becomes a shareable dashboard that can be saved, edited, and used during the trip.

## Launch

Public launch target: June 15, 2026.

## Founder Bio

Alex Teh builds AI operating systems and practical AI products for business and consumer workflows. Tripva is his travel-planning product focused on turning AI output into a real, usable trip dashboard.
